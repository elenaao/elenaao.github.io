Zoe the Bunny

Zoe the Bunny Interactive Story

User Story : This app is an interactive story. You meet this bunny named Zoe and you get on an adventure with her. After You meet her than, feed her breakfast after breakfast you walk outside to her car and drive to the aquarium. At the aquarium she see a shark and talks about the shark. Than after she talks to you about the shark she get tired and wanna to go home. User has to click on the door to take Zoe back outside to her car to drive back home.
